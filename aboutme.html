Introduction

I am a seasoned cybersecurity strategist with more than eight years of hands-on experience in network security, offensive operations, and enterprise cyber-defense architecture. My work spans malware development, red and purple team engagements, and enterprise-level security management, underpinned by CISM certification. On the blue team front, I build and maintain resilient defensive infrastructures: deploying and tuning EDR platforms, configuring firewalls, establishing IPsec tunnels, designing and hardening Active Directory, managing certificate lifecycles, automating patch management, and driving enterprise vulnerability scanning and reporting. These efforts keep preventive and detective controls sharp while ensuring compliance and operational resilience. As a malware developer and custom Command-and-Control server engineer, I craft bespoke offensive toolsets for simulation, red teaming, and post-exploitation workflows. My payloads operate stealthily in hardened environments, routinely bypassing state-of-the-art EDRs and network defense layers. During red team and pentesting operations, I specialize in adversary simulation, initial access, privilege escalation, and lateral movement across complex enterprise landscapes. In purple team engagements, I bridge red and blue efforts, closing visibility gaps, sharpening detection logic, and aligning threat modeling with incident-response playbooks. My expertise extends to firewall and encryption systems, PKI and certificate management, and holistic infrastructure security architecture. I ensure that security operations align with business objectives and regulatory mandates by blending strategic leadership with deep technical execution.
